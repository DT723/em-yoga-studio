Coaching

My goal for our coaching is for you to become fully aligned with your inner being—your true self— so you can identify your dreams and fulfill your deepest desires.

Sounds woo-woo. I know. Too good to be true. I know.

But I believe it’s possible so let’s give it a shot. We will slow down. Breathe. Recognize what is going on and, BE IN IT, allow it happen; get curious about (instead of judging or comparing), and meet whatever is coming up a kind and gentle way.

I will guide you through self-study and self-inquiry to identify the patterns and behaviors you want to change and goals you want to accomplish. Once you know what patterns you want to change, you will pay attention to when and how those patterns show up so you can get curious: What triggers them? Are they internal or external triggers? How do I best take care of myself right now?

As your coach, I’ll help you identify your passions, deepest interests, innate gifts that truly embody who they are and will bring them genuine fulfillment, purpose, etc. I’ll ask questions, offer observations, and provide tools in support of your ability to dig deep and do the powerful work of growth and development. I will support you to learn how to be more present in the moment to harness your innate power and energy to achieve your goals.
